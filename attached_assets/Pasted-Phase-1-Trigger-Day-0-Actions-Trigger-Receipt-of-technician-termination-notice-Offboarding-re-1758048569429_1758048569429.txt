Phase 1: Trigger & Day 0 Actions

Trigger: Receipt of technician termination notice → Offboarding request submitted.

Inventory Team – Day 0

Remove terminated technician’s truck from TPMS assignment.

Reconcile pending returns and receipts.

NTAO Team – Day 0

Place a shipping hold to prevent future shipments.

Cancel any pending orders.

Cancel all back orders.

Update Anaplan, Softeon, and any other replenishment systems.

Fleet Team – Day 0

Contact technician to schedule retrieval of assigned truck.

Instruct technician to:

Remove trash and personal items.

Provide interior/exterior vehicle photos.

Arrange truck transport:

To Pep Boys (preferred).

If not available, to a local repair shop.

Record vehicle details & location in AMS and Holman.

Update fleet dashboard with status.

Equipment Team – Day 0

Contact technician to reconcile tools, equipment, and iPhone.

Notify HSHRG if tool shortages exist.

Instruct technician to:

Leave tools inside truck.

Return iPhone with UPS QR code.

Cancel open orders in Segno Workflow.

Stop in-transit vendor shipments.

Suspend phone line & disable activation codes.

Update TRUCE system.

Phase 2: Day 1–5 Follow-up

Trigger: Completion of Day 0 actions → Automatically generate new Fleet checklist.

Fleet Team – Day 1–5

Retrieve & review Holman service history. Confirm last PM/oil change.

If service due, schedule PM/oil change at:

PMF

Pep Boys

Local repair shop

Capture/file updated vehicle photos.

Perform condition check using Fleet Checklist:

Tires, Brakes, Battery, Windshield, General condition.

Update fleet dashboard: set vehicle status = “Ready to Assign.”

Coordinate delivery to next technician or arrange Uber pickup.

How to Build This in Replit

Trigger Rule: Any “offboarding request” creates a Day 0 checklist.

Completion Rule: When Day 0 checklist is marked complete, auto-generate Day 1–5 checklist (Fleet only).

Assignments: Each task group (Inventory, NTAO, Fleet, Equipment) should be tagged to its team so updates are visible in your dashboard.

Status Tracking: Require completion flags in system before advancing (no Day 1–5 until Day 0 closes).
